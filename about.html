About Us

Integration Logistics Inc. is a Canadian logistics company providing flexible freight and transportation coordination for businesses across Canada.

Built for reliable execution

We support businesses with importing, exporting, and domestic distribution by coordinating the moving parts of each shipment, from documentation and terminal availability to pickup, delivery, and final confirmation.

Our mission is to provide transparent, efficient, and practical logistics solutions that help customers reduce delays, control costs, and keep their supply chains moving.

  • Reliable carrier and partner management
